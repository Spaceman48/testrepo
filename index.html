How To Bake the Pie

  • Process 1 2/3 cups (250g) plain flour, 125g unsalted butter, chilled, finely chopped and a pinch of salt in a food processor until mixture resembles breadcrumbs.
  • Whisk 1 egg, chilled and 1 tablespoon chilled water in a bowl until combined, then with food processor motor running, add to flour mixture. Process until mixture begins to form large clumps, stopping machine before mixture forms a ball.
  • Turn pastry out on to a work surface and knead gently to bring together. Form into a disc for a round tart or into a log shape for a rectangular tart. Wrap in plastic wrap and refrigerate overnight or for at least 2 hours

Ingredients

  • 400gFlour
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 Diced Apples
  • 2tbs Cinnamon
  • 50ml Melted Butter
  • 10ml Water

Preheating your oven

  • Soft and Chewey, 120 Degrees, 20 mins
  • Slight crunch and moist, 135 Degrees, 25 mins
  • Golden brown and a Slight crunch, 145 Degrees, 30 mins
  • Slight browning and Crunchy, 150 Degrees, 40 Minutes
